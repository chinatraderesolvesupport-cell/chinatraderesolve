Payment, Cancellation & Refund Policy

Working draft for prototype review. This page must be reviewed and adapted after the operator, country of establishment, hosting, payment provider and data processors are selected.

Free-access status

No service fee is charged during the current free-access phase, so there is no service-fee refund process.

Voluntary project support

A voluntary contribution is not payment for a case review and does not create priority, guaranteed work or a particular outcome. Any payment error or refund request will be handled under the selected support provider’s rules and applicable law.

Future paid services

If paid services are introduced, a separate payment, cancellation and refund policy will be published before fees are accepted.
