Privacy Policy

Working draft for prototype review. This page must be reviewed and adapted after the operator, country of establishment, hosting, payment provider and data processors are selected.

Information collected

During the free-access phase, the application stage collects contact details and a short case description. Accepted users may later be asked for a limited set of key files. Service-fee payment data is not collected. If voluntary support is enabled, it is handled by an external provider under that provider’s privacy terms.

AI-assisted processing

Documents may be processed with AI-assisted tools for organisation, comparison and drafting. Important findings and outgoing documents require human review.

Data minimisation

Clients should not submit passwords, complete payment-card numbers, access codes or documents unrelated to the dispute.

Retention and rights

Before public launch, this policy must state the operator identity, retention period, storage location, processors, transfer mechanisms, deletion procedure and applicable data-protection rights.
