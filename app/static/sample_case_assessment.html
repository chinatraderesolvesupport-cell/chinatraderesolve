Sample ChinaTradeResolve case assessment

This is a fictional example of the output format. It is not legal advice and does not describe a real client.

1Case summary

Issue:The material and contents of the goods differ from the written order.
Desired outcome:Partial refund or replacement.
Current status:A marketplace dispute is already open.

2Preliminary assessment

The available documents may support a credible specification-mismatch claim. The allegation of incorrect material appears stronger than a subjective quality complaint. If the supplier disputes the composition, an independent technical review may be required.

3Key facts and timeline

  1. The order description specifies a particular material and contents.
  2. The supplier later described the material differently.
  3. Photographs may show that agreed components are missing.

4Evidence matrix

ClaimSupporting materialsSupplier positionRisk
Wrong materialOrder, supplier messages, product photographsThe description was generalWithout technical testing, the terminology may be disputed
Missing componentInvoice, packing list, photographsNo clear responseIt must be shown that the component was part of the final specification

5Recommended strategy

  • Start with the written order specification.
  • Separate measurable mismatches from subjective complaints.
  • Request a separate written response to each point.
  • Consider an independent review of disputed technical facts.
