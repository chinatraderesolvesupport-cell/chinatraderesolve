AI Processing Notice

Working draft for prototype review. This page must be reviewed and adapted after the operator, country of establishment, hosting, payment provider and data processors are selected.

Purpose

AI-assisted tools may help organise files, identify dates and contradictions, compare statements and prepare initial drafts.

Human review

AI does not make final decisions about the case and does not communicate independently with suppliers, marketplaces or authorities.

Client choice

The application form provides a clear consent mechanism. If AI-assisted processing is declined, the application may need manual review or may not be accepted where manual capacity is unavailable.
