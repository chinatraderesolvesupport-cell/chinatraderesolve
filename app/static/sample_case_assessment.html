ChinaTradeResolve Case Assessment

This is a fictional sample showing the format of a deliverable. It is not legal advice and does not describe a real client.

1. Case Overview

Issue: Product material and configuration differ from the written order.
Requested outcome: Partial refund or replacement.
Current status: Marketplace dispute open.

2. Executive Assessment

The available documents support a credible claim regarding specification mismatch. The material allegation appears stronger than the subjective quality allegation. A technical inspection may be needed if the supplier disputes product composition.

3. Key Facts and Chronology

  1. The order description identifies a specific material and configuration.
  2. The supplier later described the material differently.
  3. Photographs appear to show missing components.

4. Evidence Matrix

AllegationSupporting evidenceSupplier positionRisk
Wrong materialOrder details; supplier message; product photosSupplier says the description was generalTerminology may be ambiguous without a technical inspection
Missing componentInvoice; packing list; photosNo clear responseNeed proof that the component was included in the final specification

5. Recommended Strategy

  • Lead with the written order description.
  • Separate measurable discrepancies from subjective quality complaints.
  • Request a specific written response to each numbered allegation.
  • Consider independent inspection for disputed technical facts.

Facts → Evidence → Risk → Strategy → Action
