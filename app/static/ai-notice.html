AI use notice

Working draft. Before a wider launch, the list of AI providers and processing conditions must be finalised.

Purpose

AI may help organise information, identify dates and contradictions, compare statements and prepare initial drafts.

Public AI assistant

The website assistant answers general questions. The service does not save the chat in its case database. A limited recent conversation is sent to the configured AI provider with response storage disabled. Do not enter confidential names, order numbers, passwords, private keys or full documents.

Human review

AI does not make final decisions and does not independently contact suppliers, marketplaces or public authorities.

Client consent

The application form contains separate consent. If AI processing is declined, the application may require manual review and will depend on available capacity.
