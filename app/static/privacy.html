Privacy policy

Working draft. Before a broad public launch, this text must be reviewed in light of the operator’s identity, place of registration, hosting and data processors used.

Data collected

At the free-application stage, contact details and a short case description are collected. After selection, a limited number of key documents may be requested. The service does not collect payment-card details.

AI processing

AI tools may be used to organise, compare and prepare drafts. Material conclusions and outgoing documents require human review.

Data minimisation

Do not send passwords, full payment-card numbers, access codes or documents unrelated to the dispute.

Retention and rights

Before a wider launch, retention periods, storage location, processors, deletion procedures and applicable data-subject rights must be finalised.
